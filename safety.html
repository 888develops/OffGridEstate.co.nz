Staying Safe When Exploring Property Online

Practical tips for protecting yourself when researching off-grid property.

1. Treat this as a demo

Off-Grid Estate NZ is a mock experience. Never send money, identification documents or confidential personal details based on information displayed here. Always cross-check with a trusted, licensed professional before committing to anything.

2. Verify who you're dealing with

  • In the real world, confirm that any agent is licensed with the appropriate authority.
  • Independently look up phone numbers and office locations, rather than relying purely on an email link.
  • Be wary of pressure to act urgently or exclusively via messaging apps.

3. Protect your personal information

  • Share only the minimum details needed at each stage.
  • Avoid sending scans of passports or driver licences unless you are certain of the recipient.
  • Use strong, unique passwords for any real property portals you use.

4. Be careful with payments

In a real transaction you should never transfer a deposit based solely on emailed bank details. Always:

  • Confirm account details by calling a known, verified office line.
  • Be suspicious of last-minute account changes or requests sent only by email.
  • Talk to your bank immediately if you think you may have sent money to the wrong place.

5. Do your own due diligence

For actual off-grid properties, independently check council rules, consent status, compliance of power/water/waste systems and access rights. This demo does not replace legal advice, building inspections or professional planning guidance.

Online safety checklist

Confirm agents and agencies independently.
Never send money based only on an email.
Protect IDs and financial information.
Use strong, unique passwords.
Get independent legal and building advice.
