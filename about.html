About Off-Grid Estate NZ

Connecting New Zealanders with thoughtful, sustainable off-grid living.

Off-Grid Estate NZ is a concept real estate marketplace focused on properties that enable low-impact, self-sufficient living. From tiny homes in native bush to lifestyle blocks with solar, rainwater and regenerative food systems, our goal is to surface the kinds of places that help people live closer to the land without sacrificing comfort or design.

This site is a demonstration experience only. The properties, pricing and agents shown are fictional and provided as mock data to showcase how an off-grid focused real estate platform could look and feel. No real-world transactions are processed and no listings here are currently for sale.

We imagine a future where:

  • Buyers can filter by real off-grid capabilities like power, water and waste systems.
  • Sellers can tell the full story of how their land is set up, beyond simple bedroom counts.
  • Agents specialising in sustainable property have tools tailored to their clients.

If you are exploring this demo as an investor, founder or collaborator and would like to talk about turning Off-Grid Estate NZ into a real product, please use the contact form on the Contact us page.
