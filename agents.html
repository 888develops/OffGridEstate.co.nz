For Agents

Tools for specialists in sustainable and off-grid property.

Off-Grid Estate NZ imagines a dedicated channel for agents who understand lifestyle blocks, regenerative farming, eco-villages and remote properties. Rather than treating these as an afterthought, the whole search experience is designed around the needs of your buyers.

What an agent profile could offer

  • Rich agent profiles highlighting your off-grid and rural experience.
  • Saved searches for buyers looking for specific system setups.
  • Story-driven listing templates for properties with a unique ethos.
  • Better context on infrastructure, access and regulatory considerations.

This page is part of a mock prototype only. If you are an agent and this resonates, we'd love to hear how you'd want a platform like this to work in practice.
