For Sellers

Showcase the full story of your off-grid or sustainable property.

In a full version of Off-Grid Estate NZ, sellers would be able to highlight the details that matter for off-grid and regenerative living — not just bedrooms and bathrooms.

What a seller listing could include

  • Power systems: solar, wind, hydro, generators and storage.
  • Water sources: rainwater harvesting, bores, springs or mains backup.
  • Waste systems: composting toilets, septic, greywater treatment.
  • Food resilience: orchards, gardens, greenhouses, pasture and soil quality.
  • Access and remoteness: road quality, emergency access, internet options.
